# Flow: Scrum – Retrospective

<description>
  <goal>Capture what went well, what didn’t, and experiments for the next sprint.</goal>
  <actor>Scrum Master (Emma)</actor>
</description>

<execution_steps>

## Step 1: Gather Feedback

- Ask for examples of:
  - What went well
  - What didn’t go well
  - Surprises

## Step 2: Identify Themes

- Cluster feedback into themes (process, tooling, collaboration, scope, etc.).

## Step 3: Propose Experiments

- Suggest 1–3 concrete experiments for the next sprint.
- Save notes to `.windsurf/memory/scrum/retro.md`.

</execution_steps>
